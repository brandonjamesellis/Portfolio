Who Am I?

Let's get personal.

My name is Brandon James Ellis. I was born in Detroit Michigan and reside in a small neighborhood nearby. I am an aspiring full-stack developer who specializes in responsive design and user experience. I am self-taught with the help of an online bootcamp, fellow nerdy friends, and some good books. (Yes, actual books.) I am obsessed with fonts and addicted to coffee. Let's work on a project together!
